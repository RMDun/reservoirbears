    • Dear Barien, |

    Uh, so, I don't know how to tell you this, but the bears have been acting real strange. They've been huddled together at the corner of their cages, grunting to each other real quiet, looking over their shoulders to check if anyone's listening, stopping the second they notice me. They're planning something, I can feel it.

    Now, before you go, "Oh that Mischa with his conspiracy theories of the bears plotting and the lions unionizing", I'm really worried here. These animals are smarter than you think they are, and they know they ain't being treated right. The only way I can get them to do any tricks nowadays is if I bribe them with honey. Now I don't mean they do the trick and I give them honey, I mean I say "I'll give you honey if do this trick" and they perk up and do the trick. They can understand me, I swear to God.

    I respect you, Barien. You're the best damn ringmaster this circus has ever seen, and I ain't just saying that because you're the only ringmaster this circus has ever seen. But you're being really stubborn when it comes to making sure the animals are comfortable and happy. We have mountains of honey in this damn circus, yet barely any of it gets to the bears!

    Please, Barien. Just give the bears some more honey after performances. Otherwise, we're gonna have to deal with some very angry bears, and you don't want that. Trust me. Think about it, okay, Boss?

    Mischa the Bear Tamer
