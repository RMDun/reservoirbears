    • Hello Mister Barien, |

    I do not know how to tell you this, but bears have been acting real strange as of late. They huddle together at corner of cage, grunting to each other, looking over shoulder to check if anybody listens. They stop doing this when they see me. They are planning something...

    Now, I know you are thinking, "Oh, that Misha with his conspiracy theories of bears plotting and lions threatening to unionize". But I am worried. Bears are smarter than you think, and they know they are being treated badly. The only way I can get them to do trick now is if I make promises of honey. I do not mean they do trick and I give honey, I mean I tell bear "I give you honey if you do trick" and they do trick! They understand me, I swear.

    I respect you, Mister Barien. You are best damn ringmaster this circus has ever seen. You are only ringmaster this circus has ever seen. But you are real stubborn when it comes to making sure bears are comfortable and happy. Even lions are better off in their hole. After all, we have mountains of honey, yet barely any gets to bears!

    Please, Mister Barien. Give bears more honey after performances. Or else bears will be angry. We do not want angry bears. Angry bears are scary. Job is already scary enough. They are bears! Also, can we please maybe talk about moving my tent away from the portable toilets?

    Misha the Bear Tamer
